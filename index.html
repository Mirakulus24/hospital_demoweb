Pelvic/Obstetric Scan

  • Drink 1 liter of water 1 hour before the exam.
  • Do not empty your bladder until the scan is complete.

Pre-Surgical Protocol

  • Fasting: No food or water 8 hours prior to surgery.

Blood & Diagnostic Tests

  • For Fasting Blood Sugar: 8–12 hours of overnight fasting.
